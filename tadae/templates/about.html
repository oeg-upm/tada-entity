Welcome to tada-entity

An online tool to automatically guess the class (the meaning) of the entity column in a give table.

Input and Output

The system takes as an input a tabule (e.g., a CSV file) and output the most probable classes ordered by the likelihood.

How to use it?

To annotate your data:click on "Annotate" (in the sidebar), it will redirect you to go to the page to upload your data.
To see the results (the annotations guessed by the system): click on "Annotations" in the sidebar, look for your data and click on the corresponding "results" link. {%endblock%}
